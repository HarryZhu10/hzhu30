Crab

Crab isn't actually a crab. Crab was a blue dragon that was abandoned by its parents at birth. Born without wings and very overweight, his parents didn't believe in his ability to carry on their legacy. They thought he would bring shame to their family. A few hours after his birth, the father dropped the poor baby dragon by the beach shore. A Devo passed by and decided to care for the lonely creature. The Devo named the blue dragon Crab because the dragon was found at the beach and it was his favorite crustacean.
